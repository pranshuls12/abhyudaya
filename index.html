About Abhyudaya

Journalism Society of MITS brings you "Abhyudaya", the fest that brings you a variety of events to showcase your skills, win over your fears and rise like the morning sun. Brace yourself and take the plunge to be a part of it on 2nd and 3rd March, 2019. Events are briefed below.

Qrious-

The competition aims at testing the general knowledge of students including interesting and fun questions to play with to be held on 2nd March 2019.

Alankrit -

Time to jot down your thoughts and weave them into a story in the creative writing contest, "Alankrit", on 2nd March 2019.

Kalakari -

Sketch out your talent in the live sketching event, draw, create and win on 3rd March 2019.
